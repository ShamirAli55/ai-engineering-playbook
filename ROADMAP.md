# 🎯 Long-Term Vision

Build one of the most comprehensive, practical, and visually engaging open-source references for modern AI engineering.

The goal is to help developers understand how modern AI systems work—from prompt design to production deployment—through clear explanations, real-world examples, high-quality diagrams, and production-focused best practices.

Ultimately, AI Engineering Playbook aims to become the go-to resource for developers, students, and engineers building modern AI applications.
